Trident

About

Trident, Hyderabad, located in the center of the HITEC City and close to the Special Economic Zone is rated amongst the best hotels in Hyderabad. It features an all-day dining restaurant with an open kitchen showcasing world cuisine, an Italian specialty restaurant with a wine-tasting room, an Indian restaurant offering private dining rooms, and a tea bar. The hotel boasts of the largest rooms in the city. It offers 323 tastefully appointed guest rooms including 30 suites. Trident Club, the Executive floor, is a private sanctuary and offers exclusive facilities and services to discerning guests. Trident Meetings, the business center, features state-of-the-art facilities with a 24-hour business center and 8 meeting rooms that accommodate up to 14 guests.
